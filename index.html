How do I determine my eligibility?

At Just-Dream Global we consider eligibility as a right. Everyone is eligible, but we investigate some standard for the sake of regulations.

  • You must be between the age of 25 - 59 years
  • You must have a running business to be eligible for business loan
  • You should either be an employee or have a stable source of income to be eligible for a personal loan
