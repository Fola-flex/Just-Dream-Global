Frequently asked questions

We have answers to your questions.

What is Just-Dream Global?

Just-Dream Global: is a fully registered company that offers both online and onsite lending services.

How do I locate your office?

We are always available at 7, ILESANMI ADEOLA STREET, BY NYSC BUS STOP, IGANDO, LAGOS STATE.

Which document do I need to get a loan?

  • Valid proof of identification
  • Proof of residency (Utility bill or bank statement)
  • Recent passport

Which type of loan does Just-Dream Global offer?

We offer business loans to registered and non registered businesses.
We as well offer personal loans.

How do I determine my eligibility?

At Just-Dream Global we consider eligibility as a right. Everyone is eligible, but we investigate some standards that meet our terms and conditions.

  • You must be between the age of 20 - 59 years
  • You must have a running business to be eligible for business loan
  • You should either be an employee or have a stable source of income to be eligible for a personal loan

How much can I borrow?

You can borrow between N50,000 - N5,000,000

How do I make repayment?

You make your loan repayment via bank transfer to the Just-Dream Global bank account with your repayment card number as description.

What happens if I fail to make a repayment?

NOTE: Just-Dream Global under the credit reporting Act 2017 and reports credit information of failed repayment of users through CreditRegistry.
Additionally, there are fees incurred as late repayment fee on every failed-repayment.

What is Just-Dream Global repayment card?

Just-Dream repayment card is a card with a unique identification card with numbers for each user. It aids the repayment of your loan through our agents or as a description when paying via bank transfer.

What is Help-Lend?

Help Lend is an investment product/program by Just-Dream Global where you invest your money and enjoy a high ROI.

How much can I invest?

You can start with a minimum of a hundred thousand Naira (₦100,000).

How do I join the Help-Lend program?

Just a few steps to register as an investor on the Just-Dream Global platform.

Is my money safe with you?

Yes, very safe and secure as we ensure all funds are collected with our insurance partners.

Is Just-Dream Global a Bank or Micro-Finance Bank?

NO, Just-Dream Global is not a bank neither is it a Micro-finance Bank, Just-Dream Global is a private limited liability company incorporated in July 2020 under Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with the registration number 1690515. We are registered to provide financial services (LENDING) to our clients. We partner with licensed payment processors and banks to ensure all payments are made and are securely settled
